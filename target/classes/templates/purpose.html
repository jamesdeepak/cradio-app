Purpose of This Application

1. Patient Education

One of the primary goals of this application is to demystify heart health. By providing clear, accurate information about cardiology, we empower patients to understand their conditions and treatment options better.

2. Resource Accessibility

Medical professionals and students need quick access to domain-specific definitions and overviews. This application serves as a concise digital handbook for fundamental cardiology concepts.

3. Digital Health Integration

This web application represents a foundational step towards a comprehensive digital health ecosystem, demonstrating how Java-based web technologies can be utilized to build secure and scalable medical applications.
