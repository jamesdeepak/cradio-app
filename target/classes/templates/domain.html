Domain: Cardiology

What is Cardiology?

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that deals with the disorders of the heart as well as some parts of the circulatory system. The field includes medical diagnosis and treatment of congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, valvular heart disease and electrophysiology.

Key Focus Areas

  • Coronary Circulation: Studying the blood vessels that supply the heart muscle.
  • Cardiac Electrophysiology: Understanding the electrical properties of the heart and treating arrhythmias.
  • Heart Failure: Managing conditions where the heart cannot pump enough blood to meet the body's needs.
  • Preventive Cardiology: Identifying risk factors to prevent cardiovascular diseases.

Why it Matters

Cardiovascular disease is the leading cause of death globally. Advanced understanding and application of cardiology are critical for extending life expectancy and improving quality of life for millions of patients.
